Our Services

SEND Tutoring

Tailored, person-centred tutoring designed for learners of all ages with special educational needs and disabilities to build confidence and unlock potential.

Functional Skills

Dedicated tutoring for teenagers and adults looking to achieve their Functional Skills qualifications in a calm, supportive, and structured environment.

EHCP Consultancy

Professional guidance and advocacy for parents and carers navigating the Education, Health and Care Plan (EHCP) application, review, or appeal process.

Visual Aid Design

Bespoke creation of high-quality visual timetables, communication aids, and learning prompts customized to support individual learning routines at home or school.
