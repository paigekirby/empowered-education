Welcome to EmpowerED

Navigating the Special Educational Needs and Disabilities (SEND) journey shouldn't feel like doing it alone. The UK education system and local authority processes can often feel overwhelming, complex, and legalistic. EmpowerED was established to change that.

Our Mission: To deliver robust, evidence-based specialist tutoring and EHCP advocacy that translates a child's lived experience into clear, professional, actionable results. We move families out of systemic confusion and into clear clarity.

Specialist SEND 1:1 Tutoring

Standard academic tutoring often focuses solely on performance and curriculum speed. At EmpowerED, our specialist tutoring views academic development through a holistic lens, prioritising emotional safety, regulation, and individual engagement.

The 12-Week Empowerment Programme

True cognitive growth and structural progress for neurodivergent learners require consistent, predictable interventions. For this reason, tutoring is structured as a premium 12-week bespoke block. This prevents a "revolving door" format and permits a structured, intentional sequence of delivery.

Core Specialisms

  • Primary Academic Foundations (Holistic primary school tutoring for English & Maths)
  • Functional Skills & Independence (Practical qualifications training alongside core life skills)
  • Executive Functioning Coaching (Mastering learning methods, memory, and time management)
  • Assistive Tech & Touch-Typing (Building independent alternative communication avenues)
  • Social Communication & Literacy (Focused phonics intervention for older struggling readers)
